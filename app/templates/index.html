What this site does

This is a learning platform for regular memory training. You can create quizzes and topics, add your own questions and answers, repeat them in sessions, track progress, and strengthen knowledge through regular recall.

What is saved

Accounts, topics, questions, sessions, answer results, and notes are stored in the database. Nothing disappears after logout.

What you can do

Create and edit topics, open a topic as a separate workspace, answer in text-based sessions, and review your progress.

How to use it

First create an account or sign in, then open topics, add questions, and start sessions for the topic you choose.

1. Sign in

Sign in first so all data is saved to your personal account.

2. Topics

Create a topic and enter it. After that, the topic workspace opens and only the selected topic is shown.

3. Sessions

Choose a topic and answer with text. Multiple-choice options are intentionally disabled for now.

4. Analytics

Progress, forgetting forecast, the achievement wall, and the insight notebook all have their own explanations.

Topics & questions

Create a topic, then click "Enter topic". A separate workspace will open where you can create, edit, and delete questions for that topic.
